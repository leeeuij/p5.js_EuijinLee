About

Euijin Lee is a designer and researcher pursuing her MFA in Design and Technology at Parsons School of Design. After studying fashion design and working in the fashion industry, she developed a deep interest in sustainability and how materials shape meaning and experience.

At Parsons, she works across design and code, creating responsive, accessible, and emotionally resonant interfaces through research, prototyping, and development.
